# Nexus Company Policies

## Data Retention Policy
All customer data is retained for 7 years after contract termination in compliance with SOC 2 Type II requirements.
Personal data subject to GDPR requests must be deleted within 30 days of a verified erasure request.

## Deal Approval Policy
Deals above $100,000 require VP of Sales approval before contract execution.
Deals above $500,000 require both VP of Sales and CFO approval.
Standard deals under $100,000 may be approved by Account Executives directly.

## Support SLA Policy
- Critical issues: 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution
- High priority: 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution
- Medium priority: 24-hour response, 72-hour resolution
- Low priority: 72-hour response, 2-week resolution

## Customer Onboarding Policy
New Enterprise customers must complete onboarding within 30 days of contract signing.
Onboarding includes: technical integration, user training, and success plan creation.
Each Enterprise customer is assigned a dedicated Customer Success Manager.

## Pricing Policy
- Starter: $125/month, up to 5 users, basic features
- Pro: $625/month, up to 25 users, advanced analytics
- Enterprise: Custom pricing, unlimited users, all features + SLA guarantees
